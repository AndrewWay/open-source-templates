Chapter 1

The Developers at the Door

You are standing in an open field west of a white house. The house is a carefully constructed masterpiece, with solid foundations, a beautiful facade matched by an elegant interior.

In fact, the house is such a masterpiece that an endless line of volunteers has taken the time to help you improve upon it.

There are two mailboxes here.

The first is mysteriously labeled Pull Requests.

The second is simply labeled issues ("as if I don't have enough of those to deal with", you think to yourself).

Both are overflowing.

  • To organize the issues box, turn to page 37.
  • To organize the pull requests box, turn to page 75.
